What we're doing

Minds Aligned is an organization working at the intersection of human potential and the responsible development of aligned AI. We believe the two are inseparable: you cannot build beneficial AI without cultivating the human capacities to guide it wisely.

Our work draws on philosophy, cognitive science, organizational practice, and the lived experience of people working alongside AI systems every day. We're not neutral observers — we're participants in this transformation, and we take that responsibility seriously.

How we work

We build relationships, not products. Our work happens through conversations, collaborations, and the long work of building shared understanding between people who care about getting this right.
